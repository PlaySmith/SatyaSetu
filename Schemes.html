Government Schemes

PM Gati Shakti National Master Plan

Introduction: Launched in October 2021, this initiative aims to enhance infrastructure development across India by integrating various transport modes for seamless logistics.

Started: October 2021

Target Beneficiaries: Logistics, manufacturing, and infrastructure sectors.

Objectives:

  • Integrate transport networks.
  • Reduce logistics costs.
  • Improve project efficiency.
  • Boost economic growth and connectivity.

Benefits:

  • Faster goods transport.
  • Economic growth.
  • Job creation.
  • Improved market access.

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)

Introduction: Launched in June 2015, PMAY aims to provide affordable housing for the urban poor by 2022.

Started: June 2015

Target Beneficiaries: Economically weaker and low-income groups.

Objectives:

  • Ensure housing for all.
  • Promote eco-friendly construction.
  • Provide basic amenities.

Benefits:

  • Improved living conditions.
  • Financial aid for housing.
  • Job creation in construction.
  • Better health outcomes.

Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan

Introduction: Announced in May 2020, this campaign promotes self-reliance by supporting local industries and entrepreneurship.

Started: May 2020

Target Beneficiaries: Small businesses, farmers, and the informal sector.

Objectives:

  • Boost local manufacturing.
  • Support SMEs and innovation.
  • Encourage economic resilience.

Benefits:

  • More jobs in local industries.
  • Less dependency on imports.
  • Economic stability.
  • Support for small businesses.
